Greg Johnson

Sports Reporter | Mercer County, New Jersey

Greg Johnson has been a professional sports journalist for more than a decade with The Trentonian and affiliated local newspapers within MediaNews Group. He covers the New York Giants, the Somerset Patriots (New York Yankees’ Double-A affiliate), high schools and colleges. His versatile reporting has taken him to major events including Super Bowl LIV in Miami and the 2026 MLB All-Star Week in Philadelphia.
